3D Graphics

To the left is an image created by the 3D graphics rendering program I made while taking CS 5400 (Graphics) last year. The graphics rendering program was built form the ground up. The image is constructed from a large array of polygons which are projected into space relative to the viewpoint, mapped onto a two dimensional plane, sorted by distance, and then drawn from farthest to closest with the shade of the color of the polygon determined by the dot product of the light source and the normal vector of each polygon.
